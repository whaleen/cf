This is a news feed aggregator built by @codefurniture. We hope to offer more in the way of sorting and filtering in the time to come.

The ultimate goals is syndicatation from select sources followed up by categorization. This way we can curate the content or put it into silos, hopefully making it easier for visitors to find things of specific interest. The source content is delivered via RSS.
